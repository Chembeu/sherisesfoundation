Who We Are

SheRises Foundation is committed to empower women and girls

SheRises Foundation is a nationally registered women and feminist led non-governmental organization in Kenya and was founded in 2022. It envisions a world where every woman and girl can thrive, supported by education, health, economic empowerment, and a society that values gender equality, peace and security, environmental sustainability, and mental well-being.

What We Do

Health & Well-being

Improve women's access to comprehensive healthcare services, including sexual reproductive health and mental health support.

Mental Health Awareness

Providing education, resources, and support to address mental health challenges faced by women and girls in our communities.

Advocacy and Policy Reform

Advocate for gender equality and women's rights through policy reform and legislative initiatives. Foster a culture of gender equality and inclusivity through community engagement and awareness campaigns.
