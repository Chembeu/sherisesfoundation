SheRises Foundation is committed to empower women and girls

SheRises Foundation is a registered women-led non-governmental organization working in the coastal counties of Kenya. At SheRises Foundation we advance the rights, dignity, and wellbeing of women and girls.

SheRises Foundation envisions a world where every woman and girl in Kenya has an opportunity to flourish in a society that values gender equality, peace and security, environmental sustainability, and mental well-being. Through our work, we champion access to education, health, economic empowerment, and leadership opportunities that enable women and girls to realize their full potential.

At SheRises Foundation, we believe that when women and girls are empowered, communities become stronger, more inclusive, and more resilient. We are committed to creating lasting change by amplifying voices, breaking barriers, and building a future where every woman and girl can rise with confidence and purpose.

Purpose that guides the work

We combine advocacy, care, and community-rooted programs so women and girls can rise with safety, confidence, and opportunity.

Vision

A future where all women and girls, regardless of their background or circumstances, have equal opportunities to thrive, lead, and influence decisions that shape peaceful, prosperous, gender-equitable, and climate-resilient societies.

Mission

SheRises Foundation empowers women and girls through education, health, economic empowerment, advocacy, and community action, with a focus on gender equality, mental well-being, climate resilience, and leadership for lasting social transformation.

Core Values

  1. Dignity and Compassion
  2. Gender Equality and Social Justice
  3. Empowerment and Leadership
  4. Integrity and Accountability
  5. Resilience and Sustainability
