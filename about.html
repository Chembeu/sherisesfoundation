About Us

Empowering women, strengthening communities

SheRises Foundation is a nationally registered women and feminist-led non-governmental organisation in Kenya, founded in 2022. We envision a world where every woman and girl can thrive — through education, health, economic empowerment, peace and security, environmental sustainability, and mental well-being.

Our work is rooted in dignity and compassion. We meet women and girls where they are — with care, practical support, and programmes that help them rebuild confidence, access services, and participate fully in community life.

Operating primarily in Kenya's coastal counties, we work side by side with communities, local leaders, and partner organisations to create lasting, community-owned change.

Our Guiding Principles

What we believe and how we work

Our mission, vision, and values are not aspirational statements — they are the daily commitments that guide every programme, partnership, and conversation.

Mission Statement

SheRises Foundation empowers women and girls through education, health, economic empowerment, advocacy, and community action, with a focus on gender equality, mental well-being, climate resilience, and leadership for lasting social transformation.

SheRises Foundation exists to empower women and girls to live with dignity, confidence, and opportunity by delivering evidence-based, community-centered, and rights-driven programmes that expand access to quality education, sexual and reproductive health services, mental health support, economic opportunities, and leadership development.

We work to advance gender equality, prevent and respond to gender-based violence, strengthen women's participation in decision-making, and promote climate-resilient, inclusive communities where women and girls are not only protected, but fully equipped to thrive.

Through partnerships, policy influence, and measurable community engagement, we aim to reach more women and girls each year, expand economic and leadership opportunities, and contribute to lasting, gender-responsive social change across Kenya.

Vision Statement

A future where all women and girls, regardless of their background or circumstances, have equal opportunities to thrive, lead, and influence decisions that shape peaceful, prosperous, gender-equitable, and climate-resilient societies.

SheRises Foundation envisions a just, inclusive, and equitable world where every woman and girl — regardless of her social, economic, geographic, or cultural background — has access to quality education, comprehensive healthcare, sustainable economic opportunities, and a safe environment that enables her to reach her full potential.

We aspire to contribute to a future where girls complete their education free from barriers, women enjoy financial independence and meaningful participation in economic life, and communities uphold gender equality as a fundamental human right. We envision societies where women and girls live free from violence, discrimination, harmful cultural practices, and systemic inequalities, while actively participating in leadership, governance, peacebuilding, and decision-making processes.

Through our work, we seek to support the empowerment of women and girls across the counties of Mombasa, Kilifi, Kwale, Taita Taveta, and Lamu in Kenya and beyond, improve access to health and mental health services, strengthen climate resilience among vulnerable communities, and foster sustainable livelihoods that promote dignity and self-reliance.

We envision communities where environmental sustainability and social justice go hand in hand, ensuring that women and girls are not only protected from the disproportionate impacts of climate change but are also recognized as leaders in developing climate solutions.

Ultimately, we envision a generation of empowered women and girls who are educated, healthy, economically secure, mentally resilient, environmentally conscious, and equipped to drive positive change within their families, communities, and nations.

Core Values

Our work is guided by dignity and compassion, gender equality and social justice, empowerment and leadership, integrity and accountability, and resilience and sustainability.

Dignity and Compassion
At SheRises Foundation, we uphold the inherent worth of every woman and girl, treating all individuals with empathy, respect, and humanity in everything we do.

Gender Equality and Social Justice
We champion equal rights, opportunities, and representation for women and girls while challenging systems, norms, and practices that perpetuate discrimination and exclusion.

Empowerment and Leadership
We believe women and girls are agents of change and invest in their knowledge, skills, confidence, and leadership to transform their lives and communities.

Integrity and Accountability
We conduct our work with honesty, transparency, ethical leadership, and responsible stewardship of resources, ensuring accountability to the communities we serve and our partners.

Resilience and Sustainability
We promote sustainable development, environmental stewardship, and climate resilience to ensure lasting benefits for current and future generations.

Our Story

Born from lived experience

SheRises Foundation was established in 2022 by a group of women who had witnessed, first-hand, the barriers facing women and girls in Kenya's coastal communities — limited access to education, persistent gender-based violence, restricted economic opportunity, and inadequate health services.

"When we say women-led, we mean it — from governance to ground-level delivery, women's voices shape everything we do."

Rather than designing programmes from the outside, our founders chose a different path: building with communities, not for them. Every initiative we run begins with listening — understanding what women and girls themselves identify as their most pressing needs.

Today, SheRises Foundation operates across multiple coastal counties, partnering with local organisations, county governments, and international allies to scale impact while staying true to our community-first roots.

Our Approach

How we create lasting change

We combine direct service delivery with systemic advocacy, always grounded in local context.

01

Community-Centred Design

Every programme begins with deep listening. We work with women and girls to understand their realities and co-design solutions that reflect their priorities, not ours.

02

Holistic Support

We address multiple dimensions of well-being simultaneously — mental health, economic opportunity, legal protection, and health — because challenges rarely come in isolation.

03

Feminist Leadership

We practise what we advocate. Our organisation is governed, managed, and staffed primarily by women, ensuring that lived experience shapes decision-making at every level.

04

Partnerships & Advocacy

Alongside direct programmes, we advocate for systemic policy change and build coalitions with like-minded organisations, amplifying community voices at county and national level.
