What We Do

Five areas, one commitment

SheRises Foundation operates across five interconnected programme areas. We believe the challenges facing women and girls are rarely isolated — poverty, poor health, violence, environmental vulnerability and security all reinforce one another. Our work addresses these realities together, not in silos.

Every programme is designed with communities, tested in the field, and iterated on the basis of what women and girls tell us is working.

Our Programmes

How we deliver impact on the ground

Each programme area combines direct service delivery with capacity building and systemic advocacy, tailored to local context.

Programme 01

Health & Well-being

We work to ensure women and girls can access the health services they need — without stigma, distance, or cost as barriers. Our health programming spans preventive care, mental health, and reproductive rights.

  • Mental health awareness and peer-support circles in communities
  • Sexual and reproductive health (SRH) education and services access
  • Maternal health support and safe delivery pathway linkages
  • GBV psychosocial support and clinical referral networks
Programme 02

Economic Empowerment

Financial independence is foundational to safety, dignity, and choice. We equip women with practical skills, business knowledge, and access to finance so they can build sustainable livelihoods on their own terms.

  • Entrepreneurship training and business development mentorship
  • Vocational skills workshops tailored to local market demand
  • Linkages to microfinance, savings groups, and credit facilities
  • Market access support and women-owned business promotion
Programme 03

Advocacy & Policy Reform

Sustainable change requires shifting the systems that perpetuate inequality. We advocate for gender-responsive policies, strengthen community accountability mechanisms, and amplify women's voices in decision-making spaces.

  • Legislative advocacy for women's rights and gender equality
  • Community-led gender equality campaigns and dialogue forums
  • Engaging county governments on gender-responsive budgeting
  • Mobilising grassroots coalitions around policy reform priorities
Programme 04

Climate & Environmental Resilience

Women in coastal Kenya are disproportionately affected by climate shocks — floods, droughts, and food insecurity. We centre women as climate leaders and support community-led adaptation and green livelihood strategies.

  • Climate literacy and environmental education for women and girls
  • Green livelihoods training: agro-ecology, clean energy, and recycling
  • Community-based disaster preparedness and resilience planning
  • Women's participation in local climate governance and action

What We Offer

Services delivered across all programmes

These cross-cutting services support women and girls at every stage of their journey with us.

Mentorship

Connecting women with experienced mentors across business, health, law, and community leadership for sustained guidance and growth.

Counselling

Trauma-informed, professional counselling services for mental health, GBV recovery, and personal resilience — delivered with confidentiality and care.

Training & Skills

Practical, hands-on training in vocational skills, digital literacy, financial management, and leadership — designed around participants' lives, not classrooms.

Community Support

Facilitated community dialogues, safe spaces, resource referrals, and network-building to strengthen solidarity and collective action at grassroots level.
